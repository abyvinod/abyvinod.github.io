Adding papers on Arxiv

Date Fri 03 February 2017 Category How To Tags Arxiv

Recently, I published one of my papers to Arxiv. You can find an informal discussion on this paper here .

There were a couple of things which I found relevant and omitted in the Arxiv's wizard. It is there on their FAQs.

  1. Arxiv does not accept .bib files. Instead, we have to upload the .bbl file. 1
  2. Arxiv does not accept .pdf files when generated using TeX. We have to upload the tex files, necessary figures, style files, and the .bbl file. 2
  3. Adding multiple items on Arxiv is a pain. The best way to accomplish this is to zip all the required files and then upload the zip. Arxiv can process zip and tar.gz.3

  1. https://arxiv.org/help/submit_tex#bibtex ↩

  2. https://arxiv.org/help/submit_tex#wegotem ↩

  3. https://arxiv.org/help/tar ↩
