What I want to do?

This is what I would like to do:

  1. A one-click send and retrieval of files from Zotero
  2. Annotate/highlight/draw on the PDF using GoodNotes on my iPad

Wait! What are these softwares?

Zotero: Zotero [zoh-TAIR-oh] is a free, easy-to-use tool to help you collect, organize, cite, and share your research sources. zotero.org

Zotfile: Zotfile is a Zotero plugin to manage your attachments: automatically rename, move, and attach PDFs (or other files) to Zotero items, sync PDFs from your Zotero library to your (mobile) PDF reader (e.g. an iPad, Android tablet, etc.) and extract annotations from PDF files. zotfile.com

GoodNotes: An iOS app that allows you to write/draw on your iPad. http://www.goodnotesapp.com/

Ok! What should I do?

Follow the instructions http://libguides.libraries.wsu.edu/c.php?g=294161&p=2088033 to allow a one-click send and retrieval of files from Zotero to GoodNotes on your iPad via Dropbox.

TL;DR

  1. Install Zotero
  2. Install ZotFile as a Add-On of Zotero
  3. Play with the settings of ZotFile (Zotero>Tools>ZotFile preferences)
  4. Make sure the folder ZotFile writes to is a Dropbox folder and the folder is available offline on iPad
  5. Right click on a Zotero item > Manage attachments > Send to tablet
  6. Import the file on GoodNotes app in your iPad and draw/annotate/highlight to your heart's content
  7. Right click on a Zotero item > Manage attachments > Get from tablet
